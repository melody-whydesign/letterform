Voice Wave Souvenir Generator

Record a voice, turn the waveform into irregular brown facade strips, add a raised logo relief, preview the 3D object, and export an STL for 3D printing. The regular long stripes form the base rhythm; the shorter brown strips encode the audio.

Ready. Click Record and speak for 3–10 seconds.
Use a high-contrast logo image, preferably transparent PNG or SVG. STL does not preserve printed colors, so this page converts dark/opaque pixels into shallow raised geometry.
Print tip: export in millimeters from your slicer. For a stronger souvenir, keep a 3–5 mm minimum base thickness and avoid very thin logo lines.
